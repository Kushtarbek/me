About Me

At 19, I took a leap of faith and left my home country of Kyrgyzstan to pursue my dreams in the United States. Embracing a new culture and leaving behind family and friends, I embarked on a journey of personal and professional growth. My path led me to Boston, where I honed in-demand skills at a community college. This educational foundation, combined with determination, opened doors to remarkable opportunities. Through the Year Up program, I completed a 6-month training followed by a 6-month internship at Harvard Business School, launching my career in software engineering. My story is one of resilience, adaptability, and the power of seizing opportunities to transform one's life.
